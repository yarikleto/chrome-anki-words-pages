Privacy Policy

Anki Words is a Chrome extension that translates selected text, saves vocabulary locally, and helps you review words with spaced repetition. This policy explains what data is handled by the extension and by the services it connects to.

Data stored locally

Anki Words stores your saved words, translations, review progress, review history, settings, recent translation results, and license status in your browser profile. This data stays on your device unless you export it or restore it yourself.

Translation and examples

When you request a translation, the selected text and language choices are sent to a translation service so the extension can return a translation. When you request example sentences, the word and language choices may be sent to an example-sentence service. These requests happen only after you ask for translation or examples.

Premium licensing and payments

If you buy or activate Anki Words Premium, payments, receipts, and related checkout data are handled by Lemon Squeezy. The extension may send your license key and a browser-profile activation identifier to Lemon Squeezy's license service to activate, validate, or deactivate Premium.

Accounts

Anki Words does not require an Anki Words account for vocabulary storage or review. The extension does not operate a hosted user database for your word list.

Data sharing

Anki Words does not sell your personal data. Data is sent only to the services needed for features you use: translation, example sentences, and Lemon Squeezy licensing or checkout.

Data deletion

You can delete saved words inside the extension. You can also remove extension data by uninstalling Anki Words from Chrome or clearing the extension's storage in the browser.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@ankiwords.app.
