License activation

Vocabulary data

Your vocabulary is stored in your local browser profile. Use export or backup tools before moving devices or clearing Chrome data.

Restricted pages

Chrome blocks extensions on browser pages such as chrome:// pages, the Chrome Web Store, and some built-in PDF viewers.

Refunds

Premium payments and receipts are handled by Lemon Squeezy. Include your order email or license key when asking for help.
